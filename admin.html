Informaion about the car

The Volvo XC60 is a luxury compact SUV produced by the Swedish car manufacturer Volvo. Here are some general information about the car:

  • First introduced in 2008 as a 2009 model year, the XC60 is now in its second generation.
  • It sits in the middle of Volvo's SUV lineup, between the smaller XC40 and the larger XC90.
  • The XC60 is known for its safety features, with advanced driver assistance systems such as adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and automatic emergency braking as standard.
  • The XC60 comes in several trims, including Momentum, R-Design, Inscription, and Polestar Engineered, each with varying levels of features and performance.
  • The XC60 offers a range of engine options, including a turbocharged four-cylinder, a turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder, and a plug-in hybrid powertrain.
  • The interior of the XC60 is known for its minimalist design, high-quality materials, and user-friendly infotainment system.
  • In terms of dimensions, the XC60 measures about 185 inches in length, 73 inches in width, and 65 inches in height. It has a wheelbase of about 112 inches.
  • The XC60 has received numerous awards over the years, including the 2018 World Car of the Year award and the 2018 North American Utility Vehicle of the Year award
